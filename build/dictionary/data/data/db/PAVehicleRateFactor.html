PAVehicleRateFactor (pc_pavehratefactor) (delegates to EffDated, RateFactor)
Description
A rate factor is a risk characteristic and its associated numeric value which might have an impact on premium. As used here rate factors are applied to base premium rather than rates. A common example of Rate Factors are the components of IRPM (individual risk premium modifier).
