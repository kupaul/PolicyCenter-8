RICededPremium (Virtual) (delegates to Extractable, SimpleEffDated)
Description
A delegate for RICededPremium, which is essentially a join entity between the reinsured risk (RIRisk), the Cost that is ceding to that risk, and the individual cedings that are given over to the multiple agreements which may reinsure the risk. The entities other than RIRisk are delegates that are implemented by entities which are specific to a line of business.
