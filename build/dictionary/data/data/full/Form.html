Form (pc_form) (delegates to EffDated)
Description
Represents an instance of a form on a policy. Forms can either be inferred by the form inference process based on a FormPattern or they can be attached directly to a policy if the forms are calculated by an external system. Form objects are eff-dated but don't make use of the EffectiveDate and ExpirationDate fields, as they apply to a given Period/Job rather than any particular point in effective time.
